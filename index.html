Jianyong He

I am a Researcher Assistant at School of Naval Architecture, Ocean & Civil Engineering, Shanghai Jiao Tong University (SJTU) . I obtained a Master degree from Zhejiang Univerity (ZJU) in 2021 and a Bachelor degree from China University of Petroleum (UPC) in 2018. I'm currently working on Fluid-Structure Interaction. Here is my CV.

Research

  • My research interest is in Ocean Engineering, using the methods of direct numerical simulation and large eddy simulation, the influence of Reynolds number, curvature radius and spacing ratio changes on the catenary risers structural flow field was studied, and the change characteristics of the amplitude and frequency of the catenary riser when it vibrated were analyzed.
